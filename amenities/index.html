Amenities of the Chalet

Our chalet is a fully equipped 4 bedroom, 2 baths, 3 story log chalet that sleeps 10 people. It has two spacious living rooms, both with wood burning fireplaces. There is decking on 3 sides, with a roofed front deck and a spacious side deck.

The chalet itself is located on 10.63 wooded acres. It is built on a ridge that overlooks a creek. The chalet is propane heated, as is the hot tub. We use well water that is filtered as it comes into the house. For those of whom it is important, we also have DirectTV. Some of the best and most favorite amenities are shown below. The full breakdown of what the chalet has is given below the pictures.

Five Person Outdoor Hot Tub

One of our favorites! It is covered, it is outside and you can't see it from the road. We recently installed a bar to allow the top to be easily taken off and put back on (a single person can do it). 

The hot tub is propane heated, usually kept at around 102 degrees, and has what some people call "good water action". In fact, two separate motors drive the jets. The button on the outside of the tub (the white circle) turns on the "turbo's". You almost need a seat belt to stay in place in some of the seats! 

If you're worried about it being outside - don't! As many people can tell you, this hot tub is brilliant ... excellent ... the bomb ... the cat's ass ... very good ... nice ...

Full Kitchen

The kitchen is a well-lit modern kitchen with everything you would expect from any home. It includes: 
  • Refrigerator
  • Microwave
  • Dishwasher
  • Stove and Oven
  • Settings for 12
  • Pots and Pans
  • Toaster
  • Hot Shot (boils a single cup of water)
Seven people can comfortably sit around the table to eat or play cards, chess, or chinese checkers. 

Downstairs

The basement is another fully functional floor with a second living room, a bedroom (with a queen size waterbed), and double door that opens to a concrete patio below the front deck. 

Master Bedroom

The master bedroom is the largest bedroom and contains a queen size bed. There is also a sliding door that opens to a crow's nest upper deck on the driveway side of the chalet. You can see the crow's nest from the picture on the main page.
