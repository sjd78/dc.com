Things to do in All Seasons

Ellicottville

(12 minutes from the chalet)

Ellicottville is always open - no matter what the season.  For a little town, there is plenty to do.  The ski shops are usually closed in the summer, but there are other little shops to visit.  Ellicottville even has a home page.  They list the local businesses and, more importantly, the events.  Click here for the Ellicottville home page.

Hot Tub

As always, the hot tub is a pleasure to be in year round.  In the summer months it runs a bit cooler, and in the winter months a bit warmer, but it always feels good.  After hiking, biking, walking, talking, eating, skiing, snow shoeing, golfing, shopping or even just sitting there, the hot tub always makes you feel better.
