Things To Do in the Fall

The Leaves

In the fall, the leaves turn, and the region is awash with brilliant colors. The view on the drive from Buffalo to Ellicottville during the fall can leave you breathless. Around the chalet is no exception. Being located in the woods, open up the front door, walk out onto the deck, and view the beautiful fall colors.

In the fall, there are many things to do in and around Ellicottville.; There is the Ellicottville Fall Festival, which regularly draws a large crowd. In fact, Holiday Valley opens their ski lifts during the festival weekend for people to ride up and walk down the slopes. It is quite a beautiful experience.

Mountain Biking and Hiking

(5 minutes from the chalet)

Some of the best ways to take in the fall foliage is to hike or bike. Hiking and biking in the fall is a little dryer and a little cooler than in the spring and summer months. It is a good way to make the best of the rest of the warmer months before winter rolls into town.

Little Rock City is also quite fun to hike around at in the fall. Sometimes the leaves hide the mud holes that "city boys" usually step into. They'll tell you, it doesn't smell good, but it give you something to talk about! Besides a little hidden mud, stick to the path, and enjoy the rocks.

Pumpkinville and the Haunted Hay Rides

(20 minutes from the chalet)

There are a few notable places around to visit during the autumn months. The Haunted Hay Rides are very scary. Pumpkinville is fun for the kids and adults alike. At pumpkinville, you can get pumpkins cheap. You can also buy apples and other fruits.
