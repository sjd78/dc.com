Welcome to the Dickerson Chalet!

Our chalet is located on 10.63 wooded acres of land in South Western New York bordered by 4,500 acres of a public state forest. It is located 10 minutes away from Ellicottville, NY. Travel time from Buffalo varies based on seasonal activity and weather, but we average a 75 minute drive time.

Come on in, come on down, stay for a weekend, stay for a week... ideal for that special vacation for one or two families (comfortably sleeps 10).
